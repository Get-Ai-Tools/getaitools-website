Why AI SaaS Tools Are Overpriced (And What They Actually Cost)

That $29/month "AI-powered" tool costs them roughly $0.02 to run for you. Here's the exact math, a breakdown of the 100x markup, and what you can do about it.

Pricing SaaS vs API Cost savings

The short version

Every "AI-powered" SaaS tool you pay $20-100/month for is doing the same thing: sending your text to Claude or GPT, getting a response back, and showing it to you. The AI part costs them fractions of a cent per task.

The markup exists to cover: servers, developers, customer support, sales and marketing, and profit. These are real costs - but they're costs that exist to serve thousands of users, not just you.

If you run the same task yourself - a Python script, an API key, one command - you pay only the raw AI cost. For most personal and freelance workloads, that's under $1/month total.
